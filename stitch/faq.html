Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Reach out to our customer support team.

Products

What materials are your candles made from?

Our candles are crafted with a natural soy wax blend, premium fragrance oils, and cotton wicks for a clean and long-lasting burn.

Are your candles eco-friendly?

Yes! We are committed to sustainability. Our soy wax is a renewable resource, our packaging is recyclable, and we strive to minimize our environmental impact in every step of our process.

How long do your candles typically burn?

Burn times vary by candle size. Our standard 8oz candles burn for approximately 40-50 hours with proper care.

Shipping & Delivery

How much does shipping cost?

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and order weight. We offer free shipping on orders over $50 within the US.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we only ship within the United States. We are hoping to expand to international shipping in the future!

How long does delivery take?

Orders are typically processed within 1-3 business days. Standard shipping takes an additional 3-5 business days for delivery.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We accept returns on unused and undamaged items within 30 days of delivery. Please visit our Returns page for full details.

How do I initiate a return?

To start a return, please contact our customer service team with your order number and the reason for the return.

Can I exchange an item?

We are happy to offer exchanges for items of equal value. Please contact customer service to arrange an exchange.
